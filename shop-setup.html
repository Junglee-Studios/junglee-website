Before You Begin — Complete These Steps First
1
Create a dedicated admin email for your TikTok Shop. This should be a company-controlled address — not tied to any individual employee — so your business always has access regardless of staff changes.
Example format: YourBrand_TTS@yourdomain.com  ·  e.g. JungleeTTS_US@jungleestudios.com
2
Use a unique password for this email — different from your TikTok app login. Store it somewhere secure (e.g. a company password manager or shared vault) so multiple authorized team members can access it.
3
Register your TikTok Shop account using that admin email and our referral link. This connects your shop to Junglee Studios from day one and ensures we can support your setup immediately.

4
Once registered, come back and complete this form. Provide the admin email you used so we have it on file — we'll need it to manage your Seller Center on your behalf.
