Why Junglee exists

Inside knowledge, brought out into the open.

JT was on the early TikTok Shop U.S. team — helped launch the Collectibles category, helped develop the seller playbooks operators still use today, and helped launch Countdown Bidding, TikTok Shop's auction mechanism.

From the inside, the gap was clear: capable brands and capable partners working hard, but without the platform context to know which signals actually drive discovery, ranking, and conversion on TikTok Shop. The platform moves differently. Junglee Studios was built to bring that context out — turning years of platform-side experience into playbooks, systems, and operating expertise that brands and creators can run.

How we work

Build your team. Or keep us. Your call.

Most TikTok Shop engagements are designed around staying. We design ours around making your team self-sufficient — so renewing with us is a choice, not a dependency.

  1. 01

    We run your shop from day one.

    Full-service TikTok Shop operations — setup, listing optimization, content strategy, affiliate program build, LIVE selling, ad management. Same daily work most agencies do, just operated by people who built the playbooks.

  2. 02

    We train your team in parallel.

    Your in-house operators sit alongside ours. We share the playbooks, the tools, the affiliate vetting frameworks, the LIVE scripts, the violation playbooks — the full operating system we use internally, adapted to your brand.

  3. 03

    Clean handoff at month 12.

    By the end of the engagement, your team owns the program. Documented systems, trained operators, established creator network, working LIVE cadence. Self-sufficient.

  4. 04

    Then you choose.

    Run it internally with your trained team — you own the playbooks, the tools, the relationships. Or keep us as your operator on a renewal that's earned, not locked in. Same caliber of work either way.
