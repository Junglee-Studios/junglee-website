How we work

Built to make your team self-sufficient.

Most TikTok Shop agencies are designed around staying. We design ours around growing your team into ownership — so by month 12, the program belongs to your brand because you've been running it with us all along.

  1. 01

    We embed with your shop from day one.

    Full-service TikTok Shop support — setup, listing optimization, content strategy, affiliate program build, LIVE selling, ad management. We run the parts your brand needs us to run, and pick up the workload your in-house team can't yet handle. Same scope as a traditional agency, structured around partnership instead of takeover.

  2. 02

    Your team operates with us, not after us.

    Your in-house brand team sits alongside ours from day one. We build playbooks tailored to your brand — affiliate vetting frameworks, LIVE scripts, violation handling, the full operating system we use internally — and your team learns the work by doing the work, side by side with ours.

  3. 03

    Ownership transfers continuously.

    No handoff event, no gap. As your team builds confidence, more of the program moves to them. By month 12, they're already running it — documented systems, trained internal team, established creator network, working LIVE cadence — because they've been running it with us the whole time.

  4. 04

    Then you choose.

    Run TikTok Shop fully internally with your trained team — you own the playbooks, the tools, the relationships. Or keep us as your partner on a renewal that's earned, not locked in. Either way, your brand is never in the position of inheriting a program you've never run.
