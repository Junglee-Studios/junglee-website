FAQ

Common questions from creators.

Everything we get asked when creators are deciding whether to apply.

What's the difference between Junglee Studios and Junglee Academy?

Junglee Studios is the parent agency — a TikTok Shop Certified Full-Service Management Partner. Junglee Academy is the coaching arm: stage-based playbooks for affiliate creators, with brand matchmaking and platform support layered in. Same team, same insider knowledge.

What does it cost to join the Academy as a creator?

The Academy itself is free — no subscription, no upsell, no paywalled content. Junglee earns only when you earn on TikTok Shop: a flat 20% commission on product sales you drive through video, Shop Tab, and LIVE as part of your Junglee partnerships.

Everything else is 100% yours — gifted product, TikTok platform rewards (Creator Rewards, Effect Creator, etc.), off-platform brand deals, paid sponsorships, your own non-Junglee affiliate links, and any other income you create on or off TikTok. We don't touch it.

And we still coach you on all of it. The Academy teaches you how to earn across every opportunity available to TikTok creators — qualifying for platform rewards, growing income streams off-platform, and getting the most out of every gift, sample, and collab that comes your way — even though we only earn on Shop sales. We're invested in your full creator business, not just the slice we get paid on.

If you don't earn on Shop, we don't earn. Simple.

What follower count do I need to apply?

You must already be signed up as a TikTok Shop Creator Affiliate (the free TikTok program separate from Junglee). 1,000+ followers gets you into TikTok Shop's pilot affiliate program; 5,000+ unlocks the full creator program. Apply through the Academy either way — we'll learn your niche, audience, and the brands you want to work with.

How are stages unlocked?

Stages unlock based on TikTok creator tier progression and performance signals — not a single GMV number. TikTok itself assigns creators tiers (L1 through L4 and higher), and our stage system maps to that progression along with consistent activity, content quality, and earning. The goal is to coach our creators to L4+ — where consistent earning compounds. As we add new playbooks and stages, members get them automatically when they qualify.

Is the Academy content protected?

Yes. All Academy content is watermarked with the member's name. Downloads are disabled, copy-paste is restricted, and printing is blocked. Your coaching content stays yours, and our IP stays ours.

How long does application review take?

Applications are reviewed within 1–2 business days. Our team reviews every application personally — once you're approved, Academy access unlocks automatically.
