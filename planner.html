FAQ

Everything we get asked first. If yours isn’t here, it’s a good first question to ask through email.

How does setup work after I subscribe?

Click subscribe on either plan, complete Stripe checkout, and you'll get a confirmation email immediately. Within 1 business day, our team sends you your Junglee Planner login and a quick onboarding email walking through how to connect your social networks. You can start planning posts the same day your account is provisioned.

Do I need a Junglee Studios Academy account to subscribe?

No. Junglee Planner is a standalone product — anyone with a credit card can subscribe, starting at $14.99/month. The Academy is our separate coaching and brand-partnership program; subscribing to Planner does not enroll you in the Academy and you don't need to be in the Academy to use Planner.

What's the difference between Planner ($14.99) and Planner Pro ($19.99)?

Planner is the everyday tier for solo creators and single-brand owners — you get the full calendar across 14 platforms, AI caption + hashtag tools, Adobe Express and Canva editors inside the app, drafts, templates, peak-time recommendations, and the mobile app. Planner Pro adds the team and analytics layer: an analytics dashboard with customizable report templates, UTM and trackable link generation, post approval workflow with collaborative notes, autolists for evergreen content, the Looker Studio connector, and bulk planning via CSV. If you're a solo creator just trying to post more consistently, pick Planner. If you're running a brand with a team — or you want real reporting on what's working — pick Pro.

Can I upgrade from Planner to Planner Pro later?

Yes. Email planner@jungleestudios.com or upgrade through your Stripe billing portal. You'll only be charged the prorated difference for the remainder of your billing cycle.

Which social networks does Junglee Planner support?

14 in total: TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, LinkedIn, Facebook, Pinterest, Threads, X / Twitter, Bluesky, Google Business Profiles, Messenger, plus paid placements on TikTok Ads, Meta Ads, and Google Ads.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel from your Stripe billing portal or email planner@jungleestudios.com. You keep access through the end of your current billing cycle, and your account becomes inactive at the next renewal date. No long-term contract, no cancellation fee.
