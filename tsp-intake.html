Getting Started
Complete these two steps before filling out the form below — they give us the access we need to manage your shop on day one.
Step 1 — Share Your Shop Code
1
Log in to TikTok Shop Seller Center at seller-us.tiktok.com using your admin email.
2
Click your account name (top right) → Account → Shop Info. Your Shop Code starts with a U (e.g. U1234567890) and appears at the top of that page.
3
Enter your Shop Code in the form below. We'll use this to submit our TSP partner request and connect your shop to Junglee Studios in TikTok's Partner Center.
Step 2 — Add Junglee Studios as an Admin in Seller Center
1
Log in to TikTok Shop Seller Center → go to My Account → Staff Management (or search "Staff" in the top bar).
2
Click Invite Staff Member and enter the Junglee Studios email assigned to your brand — this was included in your welcome email in the format yourbrand@jungleestudios.com.
3
Set the role to Admin / Operator and grant full permissions including: shop management, product management, order management, affiliate access, and ads access.
4
Click Send Invitation. We'll accept it from our end and notify you once we're in — typically within a few hours.
