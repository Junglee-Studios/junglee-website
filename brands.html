  1. 01

    We embed with your shop from day one.

    Your team operates with us, not after us.

    Your in-house brand team sits alongside ours from day one. We build playbooks tailored to your brand — affiliate vetting frameworks, LIVE scripts, violation handling, the full operating system we use internally — and your team learns the work by doing the work, side by side with ours.

    Ownership transfers continuously.

    No handoff event, no gap. As your team builds confidence, more of the program moves to them. By month 12, they're already running it — documented systems, trained internal team, established creator network, working LIVE cadence — because they've been running it with us the whole time.

  4. 04

    Then you choose.

    Run TikTok Shop fully internally with your trained team — you own the playbooks, the tools, the relationships. Or keep us as your partner on a renewal that's earned, not locked in. Either way, your brand is never in the position of inheriting a program you've never run.

FAQ

Common questions from brands.

Everything we get asked on the first call. If yours isn't here, it's a good first question on the strategy call.

Who is Junglee built for?

Established brands ready to treat TikTok Shop as a real revenue channel, not a side experiment. Typically D2C-native or D2C-ready, with product-market fit, inventory capacity, and the willingness to lean in aggressively. Smaller brands with the capability and ambition to invest meaningfully are absolutely a fit — what matters is readiness to scale, not a fixed revenue floor.

What does working with Junglee actually look like?

We work alongside your team for a full 12-month engagement, building playbooks tailored to your brand and operating the program together — shop optimization, creator commerce, content production, and LIVE selling. We don't run a black-box service; we share what we're doing, how, and why, so your team can eventually own the program. By the end, you have a documented operating model and a team that knows how to run it.

What does the first 90 days look like?

Month 1 is shop and content audit, creator-tier mapping, and infrastructure setup — getting the foundation right before we go live with creator and LIVE programs at scale. Month 2 is creator activation and content rhythm: matching your brand with creators who fit your audience and category, briefing campaigns, and starting LIVE. Month 3 is optimization — measuring what's working, doubling down on the winners, and starting the documented playbooks your team will eventually own.

What happens at the end of the 12-month engagement?

Your team owns it. The whole engagement is designed around handing over a working program, not building a permanent dependency. Most brands extend with us into a smaller advisory or LIVE-production retainer, but the core operations — shop, creators, content rhythm — should be running inside your team. If we've done it right, you don't need us the way you did at the start.

What certifications does Junglee hold?

Junglee Studios is a TikTok Shop Certified Full-Service Management Partner — the badge you see on every page of this site. Junglee was founded by Jatin "JT" Patel, who served on the founding team at TikTok Shop's U.S. launch, where he co-led the build of the Collectibles category from zero and led Countdown Bidding from concept to live. The team brings insider knowledge of how the platform actually rewards brands and creators, not third-party guesswork.

How does Junglee work with creators on behalf of our brand?

Junglee operates both sides of the marketplace — full-service brand management AND a coached creator roster through Junglee Academy. That means your campaigns get matched to creators we know, coach, and can actually deliver against a brief. Brands working with Junglee aren't pitching cold to a list of handles; they're getting matched into a network of vetted, trained affiliate creators who already understand TikTok Shop selling.

Beyond our own network, we have the ability to conduct targeted and mass outreach across TikTok's creator marketplace — reaching established, proven, and high-performing creators outside our roster to match your brand with the right talent at the right scale.

Do you only do the 12-month build program, or can you just run our TikTok Shop ongoing?

Both. The 12-month program is designed for brands who want to end up owning TikTok Shop in-house — we run it with you while transferring ownership. But if you'd rather have a long-term agency partner with no internal build required, we work that way too. We call it the extension model — we run shop, affiliate, content, and LIVE as a managed service on an ongoing basis, with monthly reporting. Most brands know which they want before the first call; we'll confirm together.

What reporting and insights do we get?

Every brand engagement includes monthly performance reporting covering GMV, affiliate channel metrics, content benchmarks, LIVE performance, and growth signals. We don't just run the program and hand you a dashboard — we interpret what's happening, where the growth levers are, and what we're doing about them.
