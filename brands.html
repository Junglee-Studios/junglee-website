Common questions

Frequently asked questions.

What does a TikTok Shop management agency do?

A TikTok Shop management agency handles all aspects of your TikTok Shop — from storefront setup and product listings to creator affiliate programs, live selling, content production, and paid ads. Junglee Studios offers full end-to-end management or strategic consulting alongside your existing team.

What is TikTok Shop Partner (TSP) status?

TikTok Shop Partner (TSP) is an official certification granted by TikTok to agencies that meet platform standards for seller support, growth management, and platform expertise. Junglee Studios holds TSP status, meaning clients receive priority support and platform access not available to general agencies.

How long does it take to scale a brand on TikTok Shop?

Most brands start seeing meaningful GMV movement within 60–90 days, once the creator engine, content rhythm, and campaign infrastructure are in place. Brands with existing audiences or strong product-market fit can see results faster.

What GMV level do brands need to work with Junglee Studios?

We typically work with brands generating or targeting $25K+ monthly GMV. We work best with established brands treating TikTok Shop as a serious revenue channel — not a side experiment.

How is Junglee Studios different from other TikTok Shop agencies?

Junglee Studios was founded by a former TikTok Shop Category Leader who helped build the platform — including launching Countdown Bidding. We hold TSP, MCN, and CAP certifications simultaneously — the only known agency to do so. We bring insider platform playbooks, not guesswork.
