FAQ

Common questions from creators.

Everything we get asked when creators are deciding whether to apply. If yours isn't here, the application process is the right next step.

What is Junglee Studios — and how is it different from a typical influencer agency?

Junglee is a TikTok Shop creator agency. Influencer agencies book one-off sponsored posts and brand deals. Junglee is built around affiliate commerce — helping you build a consistent, repeatable income stream on TikTok Shop through the creator program, brand and product matching, content coaching, LIVE, and platform support. We're certified by TikTok to formally represent creators, which unlocks tools and protections individual creators can't access on their own.

What follower count do I need to apply?

1,000+ followers gets you into TikTok Shop's pilot affiliate program. 5,000+ unlocks the full creator program. Apply through Junglee Academy either way — we'll learn your niche, audience, and the brands you want to work with, then place you in the right tier from day one.

What is the Junglee Academy and what's inside it?

The Academy is where the work happens. Inside: hook frameworks that perform on TikTok Shop, posting cadence guidance, LIVE coaching, content reviews, and the platform-side knowledge that makes the difference between viral once and earning consistently. It's also where we run brand briefs, ship samples, and unlock other monetization paths beyond pure affiliate — LIVE gifts, sponsored campaigns, brand deals, Mega Live.

How does Junglee match me with brands, and how do I get paid?

We match you with brands and products that fit your audience and content style — not just whichever brand is paying the most that week. You get briefed on the campaign, ship samples, and create. You get paid through TikTok Shop's affiliate commission system on every sale you drive, plus any negotiated rate uplifts we secure on top. Where solo creators see standard marketplace commission, agencies like Junglee can negotiate higher rates with brands and pass them through to you.

Beyond commission, what other ways can I earn?

LIVE gifts during streams, sponsored campaigns from brand partners, brand deals where you're the named talent, and Mega Live opportunities — the multi-creator brand events that move serious volume. Inside Junglee Academy, we coach you on how to build content that earns from multiple streams at once, not just commission.

What does Junglee actually do for me that I couldn't do alone?

Three things. First, platform leverage — we have direct relationships and certifications that unlock support, recovery from violations, and policy clarity that creators can't get on their own. Second, brand access — we run campaigns from brands actively spending on TikTok Shop, and place those campaigns inside our network rather than letting creators pitch cold. Third, coaching from people who built the platform — JT was on the founding team at TikTok Shop's U.S. launch, and we know exactly what discovery rewards and how LIVE actually performs.
