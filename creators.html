Common questions

Frequently asked questions.

What is TikTok Shop for Creators?

TikTok Shop for Creators is TikTok's affiliate program that lets you earn commissions by promoting products in your videos and lives. You don't hold inventory or handle shipping — you simply create content around products and earn a percentage of every sale you drive. Junglee Studios is an official TikTok MCN (Multi-Channel Network), meaning we're certified by TikTok to formally represent your account, protect it, and unlock opportunities individual creators can't access on their own.

How do I earn money as a TikTok Shop affiliate?

You earn a commission on every sale that comes through your content — videos, lives, or your storefront. The more consistent and targeted your content, the more you earn. As a Junglee Studios creator, you also get early access to brand campaigns, free product samples, and performance coaching that helps you find and scale your winning formats faster.

Do I need a large following to join?

Our standard program requires a minimum of 5,000 followers. If you're earlier in your journey, we also offer a pilot program open to creators with at least 1,000 followers. We also provide account growth coaching for creators who want to build their audience alongside their TikTok Shop income.

What is the difference between a creator agency and an influencer agency?

An influencer agency typically books sponsored posts and deals for large creators. A creator agency partner like Junglee Studios is officially certified by TikTok to grow creators through affiliate commerce — focusing on consistent TikTok Shop income, not one-off brand deals.
