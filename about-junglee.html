Founder

Meet Jatin Patel “JT”

Junglee Studios was founded by JT Patel, a former TikTok Shop Category Leader who helped shape how social commerce operates today.

At TikTok Shop US, JT built and scaled the Collectibles category from launch to a $750M+ GMV business, leading platform strategy across sellers, live commerce, monetization, and analytics — including spearheading the launch of TikTok Shop Auctions. Prior to TikTok, he held leadership roles at Amazon, gaining deep marketplace and category experience.

After seeing how often brands were left to “figure it out” inside complex platforms, JT founded Junglee Studios to share the real playbooks built inside TikTok and Amazon — helping brands scale correctly, build internal teams, and understand where social commerce is headed.

Highlights

  • Spearheaded TikTok Shop Auctions and helped shape key commerce features

  • Built Junglee Studios to bring platform-grade playbooks to brands and creators — with in-house enablement
