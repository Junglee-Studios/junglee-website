Meet Jatin Patel "JT"

Junglee Studios was founded by JT Patel, a former TikTok Shop Category Leader who helped shape how social commerce operates at scale.

At TikTok Shop US, JT built and scaled the Collectibles category from launch to a $750M+ GMV business, leading platform strategy across sellers, live commerce, monetization, and analytics — including spearheading the launch of Countdown Bidding on TikTok Shop. Prior to TikTok, he held leadership roles at Amazon, gaining deep marketplace and category experience.

After seeing how often brands were left to figure it out inside complex platforms, JT founded Junglee Studios to bring real playbooks from TikTok and Amazon to brands and creators — helping them scale correctly, build stronger systems, and better understand where social commerce is headed.

JT built Junglee on a simple belief: the best agency is one that works itself out of a job. Every engagement is designed to build your team's capability — not your reliance on ours. Your team, your brand, our system.

  • Spearheaded the launch of Countdown Bidding on TikTok Shop — the platform's live auction selling mechanic
  • Led platform strategy across sellers, live commerce, monetization, and analytics
