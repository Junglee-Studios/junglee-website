Meet Jatin Patel "JT"

Junglee Studios was founded by JT Patel, a former TikTok Shop Category Leader who helped shape how social commerce operates at scale.

At TikTok Shop US, JT built and scaled the Collectibles category from launch to a $750M+ GMV business, leading platform strategy across sellers, live commerce, monetization, and analytics — including spearheading the launch of TikTok Shop Auctions. Prior to TikTok, he held leadership roles at Amazon, gaining deep marketplace and category experience.

After seeing how often brands were left to figure it out inside complex platforms, JT founded Junglee Studios to bring real playbooks from TikTok and Amazon to brands and creators — helping them scale correctly, build stronger systems, and better understand where social commerce is headed.
