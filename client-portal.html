How to approve Junglee Studios as your TikTok Shop Service Partner

  1. Log in to TikTok Shop Seller Center at seller-us.tiktok.com
  2. Go to My Account → Service Providers (or search "Service Providers" in the top bar)
  3. You'll see a pending approval request from Junglee Studios — we submit this from our Partner Center once you enroll
  4. Click Approve on the Junglee Studios request
  5. Select the permissions requested (shop management, affiliate access, ads access as applicable to your program)
  6. Click Confirm — you'll receive a confirmation email and we'll be notified immediately
